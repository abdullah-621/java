Introduction to the U.S.A. Economy

  • The United States has one of the largest and most influential economies in the world
  • It is a high-income, developed country with a mixed market economy
  • The economy is driven by innovation, technology, and a large consumer market
  • Economic changes in the U.S. often impact the global economy

Key Insight: The USA economy serves as a major driver and indicator of global economic health

Inflation

What is Inflation?

Inflation is the general increase in prices of goods and services over time.

Key Effects:

  • Decreases value of money over time
  • Affects daily life and cost of living
  • High inflation hurts low-income people
  • Low and stable inflation supports growth

Financial Stability

What is Financial Stability?

Financial stability means a strong and well-functioning financial system without major disruptions.

Key Components:

  • Financial stability ensures strong banking system
  • Builds trust in banks and financial markets
  • Financial crises harm economic growth
  • Fed and government regulate financial system
